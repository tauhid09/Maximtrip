Best time to book Kashmir Honeymoon Trip Choosing the perfect time for your honeymoon trip is essential to guaranteeing a romantic and unforgettable vacation. Kashmir, renowned for its calm lakes, snow-capped mountains, and stunning scenery, has various allures all year round. Here we will assist you in selecting the ideal time for your honeymoon trip to Kashmir. Spring (March - May): Spring in Kashmir, mostly starts from March till May, when weather gets pleasant and brings new life and colors to Kashmir beauty. The valley blooms with vibrant flowers, creating a picturesque backdrop for romantic strolls. The weather during spring is pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 25°C, making it ideal for outdoor activities like boating on Dal Lake or exploring the Mughal gardens with your spouse. Summer (June - August): Summer is the peak tourist season in Kashmir, attracting honeymooners and visitor alike. With temperatures averaging between 20°C to 30°C, the weather remains cool and relaxed. This is the perfect time to book your honeymoon tour as it offers opportunities for adventurous activities such as trekking in Pahalgam or Gulmarg or enjoying a romantic Shikara-ride on the tranquil waters of Dal Lake. Autumn (September - November): Autumn transforms Kashmir into a canvas of golden hues as the leaves turn into shades of amber and scarlet. The weather begins to cool down, with temperatures ranging from 10°C to 20°C, creating a cozy atmosphere for newlyweds. This season is ideal for exploring the vibrant local markets, exploring the rich culture, and enjoying quality time in the middle of the majestic Himalayas. Winter (December - February): For couples who love the idea of a snowy heaven, winter season is best for honeymoon in Kashmir. From December to February, the valley is covered in snow, offering mesmerizing vistas straight out of a fairy tale. The temperatures drops below freezing, providing unique opportunities for skiing in Gulmarg and also a romantic stay in a cozy houseboat on Dal Lake.
